Crafting

Apricorns, Poké Ball tiers, specialty balls, and every other crafting system on the server.

Hand-written page — lives in content/gameplay/items/crafting.html, safe from the generators. Recipes below are cross-checked against the official Cobblemon Wiki; anything server-specific is marked.

Apricorns — the core catching resource

Apricorns grow in seven colors (red, yellow, green, blue, pink, black, white) on apricorn trees. Right-click a fully grown apricorn to harvest one — it regrows from a bud, taking about one Minecraft day, and harvesting has a ~10% chance to also drop a plantable apricorn seed. Don't break unripe buds: they drop nothing. Bone meal speeds growth. A small farm of each color near your base pays for itself fast.
