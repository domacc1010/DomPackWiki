Legendary Monuments

31 legendary/mythical sites across all 4 regions — 12 dedicated monuments + 19 named landmark sites.

Auto-generated from every region pack's worldgen files — now covering all four regions, including named landmark sites (Sky Pillar, Spear Pillar, Bell Tower…) that previous versions of this page missed. Landmark→Pokémon associations are grounded in the pack's own radar-crafting recipes plus Pokémon canon; confirm any you're unsure of in-game.

What is it?

Legendary and Mythical Pokémon in CobbleVerse each guard a unique structure that generates naturally in the world, rather than only spawning via events. Spacing/Separation are vanilla structure-placement values (in chunks) — higher spacing means rarer. Watch the dimension badges: some sites are in the Nether or The End.

How do I find them?

Two tools, both from LumyMon: craftable radar items tuned to a specific legendary (about 30 recipes across the regions), and Cartographer treasure maps that point straight at gyms and league towers to get you into the right area.

Crafting Locked Regions

The Rule: You cannot craft items for a region until you have officially "unlocked" it.

The Trigger: Defeating the final Champion of your current region triggers the game to unlock the next region's content.

The Result: Once that Champion falls, the next region's legendary trackers and monument maps become craftable in your JEI recipe book.

Note on regions below: groupings reflect which datapack/build area a site is bundled into on this server, not always the Pokémon's mainline-game region — Calyrex and Necrozma's sites are bonus content tucked into the Kanto-area files rather than native to Kanto.
