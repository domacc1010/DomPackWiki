LumyMon

36 treasure-map trades across 4 regions — Kanto, Johto, Hoenn, Sinnoh.

Original content — no external wiki documents LumyMon. Auto-generated from lumymon/trades — 36 treasure map trades across 4 regional cartographers.

What is it?

LumyMon adds Cartographer NPCs (one per region) who trade themed "tome" items for treasure maps pointing straight at gym leaders, legendary sites, and league towers.

Where do I find it?

Where each regional Cartographer NPC is physically located.

How do I use it?

Bring the NPC the tome item shown below (plus a vanilla map) to buy a treasure map to that destination.
